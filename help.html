Cosmic Catalyst - Help

Your guide to surviving the cosmic onslaught

Game Controls

Movement: WASD keys or Arrow keys
Shoot: Mouse click or Spacebar
Toggle Control Scheme: C key
Restart Game: ENTER key (when game is over)
Tip: Both WASD and Arrow keys work simultaneously for movement. The C key toggles which control scheme is displayed in the HUD.

Game Objectives

Survive as long as possible while destroying enemy ships to earn points. The game gets progressively harder with each wave and level.

  • Destroy enemy ships to earn points
  • Avoid enemy projectiles
  • Collect power-ups to enhance your ship
  • Survive as many waves and levels as possible

Power-ups

Various power-ups will appear throughout the game:

  • Health Boost: Restores your ship's health
  • Shield: Provides temporary invulnerability
  • Weapon Upgrade: Enhances your ship's firepower
  • Speed Boost: Increases your ship's movement speed

Enemies

Different types of enemy ships will appear, each with unique behaviors:

  • Basic Enemies: Move in predictable patterns and fire simple projectiles
  • Advanced Enemies: Appear in later levels with more complex movement and attack patterns
  • Boss Ships: Powerful enemies that appear at the end of certain levels
